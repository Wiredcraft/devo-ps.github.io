We help you manage your servers and what runs on it.

Spin off new servers on EC2, Rackspace or your own cloud, install and configure services and deploy your applications. All in one place.

How does it work?

  • Add and set up servers

    Simply spin off new instance using your AWS, Rackspace or Linode account or manually hook any server to devo.ps. You can then manage services; add, remove or configure MySQL, Varnish, RabbitMQ or any of our supported technologies.

  • Deploy your apps

    Once your infrastructure is set up, you can deploy your applications on it, leveraging the various technologies you have made available; MongoDB, CouchDB, PHP, node.js or Python.

  • Automate your jobs

    Leverage devo.ps actions for each technology to quickly build series of tasks (aka "jobs"). Trigger these jobs on various events (cron, git repository update, SMS input) for both servers and applications.

This makes us sad pandas, but devo.ps is not planned to be publicly released before middle of November 2012. In the meantime, you can stay tuned through our mailing list or follow us on twitter.
